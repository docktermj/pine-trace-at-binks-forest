# Parliamentary Procedure

## Example Agenda

1. Call to order
1. Approval and/or correction of minutes
1. Officer Reports
    1. President
    1. Vice President
    1. Treasurer
    1. Secretary
1. Committee Reports
1. Old business
1. New business
1. Announcements
1. Adjournment

## Call to order

*Note*: The term "President" may also mean "Acting President" in the President's absence.

**President:**
> The meeting will come to order at 6:35pm.

## Approval and/or correction of minutes

1. Options:
    1. The minutes may be read aloud.
    1. The President asks if the minutes have already been read by the board.
    1. The President allows time for the board members to review the minutes.

1. If no corrections,

    **President:**
    > Do I have a motion to accept the minutes as read?

    **Board Member 1:**
    > I move to accept the minutes as read.

    **Board Member 2:**
    > I second the motion.

    **President:**
    > Those in favor say 'Aye'?

    **Board Members:**
    > Aye

    **President:**
    > Those opposed 'No'?

1. If corrections are needed,
    1. Discuss the corrections and come to agreement on each correction.
